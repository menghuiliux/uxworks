About Me

I am Menghui Li, an experienced UX Designer skilled in designing comprehensive web apps, SaaS products, e-commerce websites, and mobile apps. I have a strong background in working within AGILE teams and SAFe environments, collaborating closely with developers, QA, and stakeholders to deliver seamless user experiences. With a keen eye for detail and a problem-solving mindset, I specialize in wireframe prototyping and creating pixel-perfect interfaces.

Currently, I am a Senior UX Designer at Toitū Te Whenua Land Information New Zealand, working on modernizing the country’s property system, Landonline. Previously, I contributed to Arlo Software, enhancing key SaaS product features, and served as a Design Director at Gawen & Janos, leading a team on multiple game media products.

I hold a Master of Industrial Design from Georgia Institute of Technology and a Bachelor of Engineering in Industrial and Product Design from Beijing University of Post and Telecommunications.
